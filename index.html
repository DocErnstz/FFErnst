About Me

Hello! My name is Ernesto and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2021 when I saw a video of a guy doing super cool css animations which dazzle me. due to this event, I decided to set out on a journey to built webpages as cool as him using HTML & CSS!

Even though I have never worked with a professional developer team I've been developing software for indie games with an amateur team with Godot && unity and even built a single economy simulator on my own. working with a vast variety of technologies/languages/algorithms is something which I enjoy really much since I must analyze diverse sources like blogs, docs, books, etc to get a good insight of the way I am going to approach the development process.
